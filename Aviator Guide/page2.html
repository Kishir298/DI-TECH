Engineering Colleges and Pilot Training Centers

Premier UAE institutions for aerospace engineering and professional pilot training

Pilot Training Centers in UAE

UAE Aviation Academy (UAAA)

Location: Sharjah International Airport, UAE

Highlights: Owned by the Sharjah Airport International Free Zone, UAAA is one of the most prestigious flight schools in the Middle East. Offers integrated ATPL programs, modular training, and specialized type ratings.

Aircraft Fleet: Cessna 172, Piper Seminole, Diamond DA40/42, multi-engine trainers

Partnerships: Collaborations with Emirates Aviation University and international airlines

Emirates Aviation University

Location: Dubai Academic City, UAE

Programs: Bachelor of Science in Aviation Management, Aeronautical Engineering, and Aviation Maintenance. Flight training integrated with degree programs.

Training: Private and commercial pilot licenses, instrument ratings, multi-engine ratings

Advantage: Direct pathway to Emirates Airlines careers

CAE Oxford Aviation Academy - Dubai

Location: Al Maktoum International Airport (DWC)

Reputation: Part of the global CAE network, world largest aviation training provider

Programs: Integrated ATPL, modular CPL/IR, MCC/JOC courses, type ratings

Fleet: Modern training aircraft with glass cockpits

Phoenix Aviation Academy

Location: Ras Al Khaimah International Airport

Specialization: Professional pilot training, airline cadet programs, helicopter training

Approach: Personalized training with smaller class sizes

Fleet: Single and multi-engine piston aircraft

Skyline University Aviation Academy

Location: Sharjah Airport

Programs: Commercial pilot training, private pilot licenses, aviation management degrees

Focus: Academic excellence combined with practical flight experience

Facilities: Modern classrooms, advanced simulators, dedicated flight operations

Aerospace Engineering Colleges

Khalifa University - Aerospace Engineering

Location: Abu Dhabi, UAE

Ranking: Top engineering university in the UAE and Middle East

Programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Aerospace Engineering
  • Master of Science in Aerospace Engineering
  • PhD in Aerospace Engineering

Specializations: Aerodynamics, propulsion, structures, space systems, unmanned aerial vehicles

Research: State-of-the-art wind tunnels, composite materials labs, satellite development programs

American University of Sharjah (AUS)

Department: Mechanical Engineering and Aerospace

Programs: BS in Mechanical Engineering with aerospace concentration, MS in Mechanical Engineering

Facilities: Aerodynamics laboratory, propulsion systems lab, materials testing facilities

Industry Links: STRATA Manufacturing (Mla), Airbus, Boeing partnerships

Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence

Focus: AI applications in aerospace and autonomous systems

Programs: MS and PhD in Machine Learning, Computer Vision relevant to aerospace

Application: Autonomous drones, satellite imagery analysis, air traffic management AI

Abu Dhabi Polytechnic - Higher Colleges of Technology

Location: Abu Dhabi, UAE

Programs: Bachelor of Engineering Technology in Aerospace

Practical Focus: Hands-on training with industry-standard equipment

Career Path: Aircraft maintenance engineers, manufacturing specialists, quality control

Industry Partners: Etihad Airways Engineering, Strata, Mubadala Aerospace

Selection Criteria and Fees

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma with Mathematics and Physics
  • Age: Minimum 17 years for PPL, 18 for CPL
  • Class 1 Medical Certificate
  • ICAO English Level 4 or higher
  • Pass entrance exams (mathematics, physics, aptitude)
  • Personal interview

Estimated Training Costs

  • Private Pilot License: AED 35,000 - 50,000
  • Commercial Pilot License: AED 180,000 - 250,000
  • Integrated ATPL Program: AED 400,000 - 650,000
  • Type Rating (Boeing/Airbus): AED 80,000 - 150,000
  • Bachelor Degree: AED 120,000 - 200,000/year

Note: Cadet programs often available with airline sponsorship
